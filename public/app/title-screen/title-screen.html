Hops Academy

Welcome to Hops Academy! The game where you learn the general alcoholic content of beer by answering various trivia questions. Each round is 1 question and the strength of the brew affects your ability to answer the questions. You will choose from a selection of three beers, and the alcoholic content will not be revealed until after your selection.
